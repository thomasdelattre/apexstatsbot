===== Apex stats bot =====

Discord bot for stats in Apex legends game.

Add it to your channel following this link: https://discordapp.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=552879986793775106&permissions=0&scope=bot
After that, just type "!apexstats USERNAME" in the discord channel and that's all !

Apex stats bot is taking data from https://public-api.tracker.gg
